Office Buildings & Coworking Spaces

Know more about the place you are in.

  • Employees induction
  • Augmenting Rooms Interactions and Scheduling
  • Mapping and Augmenting of desks based on who works there
  • Improve Building Manager - Employee communication

AR is great for explaining things about the buildings you are in. Working spaces usually have not only a high surface but also a high number of people coming and going, that are very dynamic (scheduling rooms, interacting with calendars, kitchen, changing desks etc.). AR could be the missing link in the communication between the the building and the end user. We strongly believe it's still has a huge unexplored potential especially in industries such as real estate. And the most importat piece for this, localization :) .

InsidAR was part of one of BoB's showcase within Commons Coworking Spaces. It was basically an AR medium between Coworking Spaces and their members. More exactly we worked on enabling location based experiences such as a Kitchen wall with a sticky board and shared calendar and a member wall in 2 different locations.
